Family Fun Day on the Trail

June 10 from 10am - 5pm at South Tama Rec Trail, Tama-Toledo, IA

Join your friends and neighbors on the South Tama Rec Trail
for Family Fun Day on June 10, 2023!

Our activities and schedule of events are subject to change.
Check this page for updates as June 10, 2023 approaches.

Directions and a list of scavenger hunt items is available to download using the "dinosaur" QR code in the poster, and paper copies will be available at the check-in station on June 10.
